Frequently Asked
Questions

What if I move in, and the flat is not as advertised?

We thoroughly check flats before listing them, and right before you move in. Still, we understand that bad surprises may happen. If you move in, and your flat is unfit for living or not as advertised, then let us know within 48 hours of moving in. We will do our best to fix any issues you are having, or if you prefer, give you a full refund, provide you with short term accommodation, and sort out moving your things from the flat that didn’t work out to a new flat.

Who do I talk to if I have issues when living in a Kiko flat?

You’re not on your own after you’ve moved in. Our support team will be there for you for the entire time you live in a Kiko flat. If any issues occur, be they technical fixes or problems with your landlord, just let us know and one of our help agents will help you fix what has been bothering you.

When do I pay for my flat?

When your offer is accepted by your new landlord, you will be charged a holding deposit of one weeks’ rent, to verify your identity and your seriousness. If you fail our reference check, then the holding deposit will be fully reimbursed to you. If everything goes well and you pass our reference check, then the holding deposit will be part of your first rent payment, which you pay only once you move in. You also pick the day of the month when you pay rent, so forget about worrying if your salary hits before rent due day. .

How does deposit free renting work?

Until now, you had two choices when renting a flat. Pay out a cash deposit of up to six weeks’ rent, or pay nonrefundable fees to a firm letting you rent with no traditional deposit. With Kiko, there are absolutely no fees you pay, and you don’t need to pay out any deposit. You still need to take care of your flat, though - you will have to pay for any damages you cause at the end of your stay.

Who checks the flats on Kiko?

We don’t cut corners when reviewing flats before listing them, because we don’t cut corners with the safety of our clients. All our flats are reviewed by professional inventory checkers and professional photographers, and they must meet our rigorous standards to be published on Kiko.
